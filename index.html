About Lort

Lort is a Rogue-like game with a silly/funny style with up to 8 player multiplayer, In Lort there are different characters and ways to play, for example you can play as a Wizard with great Area of Effect abilities, another example is an assin with good mobility, there are also many types of weapons that you can find and they are all compatible with every character. The game gets harder as you move from world to world, but if you play correctly you can scale yourself really quickly, you can collect

attributes

that give buffs toward thier type (intelligence, strength, agility) along with

items

that give you different buffs per item, there are also permanant upgrades that stay effect every run that give simple upgrades like health and starting gold. This combination of characters, item upgrades, weapon types, attributes, has 8 player multiplayer, and silly style, keeps runs fresh and fun, Lort also goes on sale often with most events



This is why lort is a really good choice if you are planning on buying a roguelike, its refreshing, goes on sale often, and is really fun





Lort Was Designed and developed by Distract-A-Shark and has an official
